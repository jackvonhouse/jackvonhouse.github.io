Architecture

Most of the systems with similar functionality have a monolithic architecture, it was decided to try divide a monolithic application into services with a specific functionality The gRPC protocol is used to transfer requests. Why? Faster, safer and more beautiful instead of jRPC and others

gRPC webhooks used to update the information in many microservices
